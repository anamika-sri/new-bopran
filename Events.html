Events & Challenges

Across the course of the year the Boparan Charitable trust hosts a number of Events and Challenges to raise funds and awareness about the work that the charity does. Without people attending our events and making kind donations, we would not be able to continue to support the children who need our support.

Northampton Abseil

2023

Various dates across 2023 / 2024

The ultimate adrenaline challenge, as you take to the top of the worlds tallest permanent abseil structure. Measuring over 400ft in height, making it taller than Big Ben, higher than the London Eye you will swing down the tower and gain the bragging right for ultimate adrenaline challenge.

2022 Highlights

We hosted our 9th Brightstar Ball where over 700 guests joined us for an evening of fundraising and entertainment. After a 3 year break we were overwhelmed to raise a staggering £1.3 million pounds for The Boparan Charitable Trust.

20 teams battled it out at Leicester City Football Club’s King Power Stadium for our annual 5-a-side football tournament. Raising over £30,000 for the Trust!

52 Golfers took on the world famous Barbizon course for a 4 ball.
